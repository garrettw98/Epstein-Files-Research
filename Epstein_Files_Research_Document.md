# PART 1: LEGALLY ESTABLISHED FACTS (✓)

## 1.1 Jeffrey Epstein - Criminal History

### 2008 Florida Case
- **Charges**: Epstein was investigated for sexually abusing dozens of underage girls in Palm Beach, Florida
- **Plea Deal**: Pleaded guilty to state charges of procuring a person under 18 for prostitution and felony solicitation of prostitution
- **Sentence**: 18 months in county jail (served 13 months with work release privileges allowing him to leave jail 6 days a week, 12 hours a day)
- **Registration**: Required to register as a sex offender
- **Non-Prosecution Agreement (NPA)**: Controversial federal deal negotiated by then-U.S. Attorney Alexander Acosta that granted immunity to "any potential co-conspirators"
- **Victims identified at the time**: At least 36 underage girls

### 2019 Federal Case
- **Date of Arrest**: July 6, 2019, at Teterboro Airport in New Jersey
- **Charges**: Sex trafficking of minors and conspiracy to commit sex trafficking
- **Jurisdictions**: Southern District of New York (SDNY)
- **Evidence seized**: Hundreds/thousands of photographs including images of nude underage girls, CDs with handwritten labels including "Young [Name] + [Name]," compact discs, computers
- **Bail**: Denied despite offering $100+ million package

### Death
- **Date**: August 10, 2019
- **Location**: Metropolitan Correctional Center (MCC), New York
- **Official Ruling**: Suicide by hanging
- **Medical Examiner**: Dr. Barbara Sampson ruled death a suicide
- **Age at death**: 66 years old
