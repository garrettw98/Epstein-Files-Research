# Primary Sources Index (Epstein Research)

> **Purpose**: A high-confidence index of source hubs and official records for this repository.
> **Updated**: February 14, 2026
> **Use Rule**: Treat official documents and court filings as primary; treat media as secondary context unless independently corroborated.

## Evidence Tiers

- **Tier 1 (Primary / Official)**: DOJ releases, court records, federal law text, inspector general reports.
- **Tier 2 (Major Reporting)**: AP, Reuters, Miami Herald, other established outlets with documented sourcing.
- **Tier 3 (Claims / Allegations)**: Statements by politicians, attorneys, or witnesses not yet corroborated by primary documents.

## Source Quality Rules for This Repo

- Prefer a Tier 1 source for factual claims about crimes, prosecutions, court outcomes, or government actions.
- If only Tier 2 is available, phrase as "reported by X" and avoid asserting intent as fact.
- Keep allegations clearly labeled and separate from findings.
- Add specific dates to every time-sensitive update.
