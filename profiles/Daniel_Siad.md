> **Status**: **ENABLER** (2026 Revelations)

## Summary

Daniel Siad is identified in the 2026 document release as one of Jeffrey Epstein's so-called **"scouts"**—individuals who identified and recruited women for Epstein's network. His emails specifically reference recruiting women from Poland, making him a key figure in understanding the **Eastern European trafficking pipeline**.

## The 2026 Revelations

Polish Prime Minister Donald Tusk cited specific documents in announcing Poland's investigation:

**2009 Email from Siad to Epstein**:
> "has some girls allready [sic]"

The email specifically mentioned:

### The "Scout" System

Siad's emails reveal how Epstein's recruitment network operated:

1. **Geographic Scouts**: Individuals stationed in or traveling to specific regions
2. **Pre-Selection**: Scouts identified women before Epstein's involvement
3. **Communication**: Regular email updates to Epstein about "girls"
4. **Pipeline**: Funneling women from Eastern Europe to Epstein's properties
