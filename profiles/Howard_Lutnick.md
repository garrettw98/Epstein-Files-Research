## Summary

Howard Lutnick is the CEO of Cantor Fitzgerald and BGC Partners, and currently serves as U.S. Secretary of Commerce in the Trump administration. The January 2026 document release revealed he visited Jeffrey Epstein's private Caribbean island with his family, directly contradicting his prior public statements that he had cut ties with Epstein "decades ago."

## The 2026 Revelations

### The Island Visit (December 2012)

Documents released by the DOJ show that in December 2012, Epstein invited Lutnick to his private island in the Caribbean for lunch.

**Key Email Exchange**:
- Epstein's invitation was enthusiastically accepted by **Allison Lutnick** (Howard's wife)
- She confirmed they would arrive **on a yacht with their children**
- This was a family visit to Epstein's island—not a brief business meeting

### Earlier Contact (2011)

- In 2011, the two men "had drinks" together
- That same year, **Epstein contributed to a philanthropic dinner honoring Lutnick**

### Neighborly Relations (2010)

- Emails from 2010 show the two men discussing **construction of a building across the street from both of their homes**
- This indicates they were neighbors and had an ongoing relationship
