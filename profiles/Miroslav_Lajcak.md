## Summary

Miroslav Lajcak is a veteran Slovak diplomat who served as President of the United Nations General Assembly (2017-2018) and held multiple senior positions in Slovak government. Following the January 2026 document release revealing his contact with Jeffrey Epstein, he became the **first major political casualty** of the files, resigning from his position as National Security Adviser on February 1, 2026.
