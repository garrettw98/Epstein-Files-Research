# Tova Noel (MCC Correctional Officer)

> **Status**: **CUSTODY-CASE FIGURE**

## Summary

Tova Noel is one of the two MCC correctional officers charged in November 2019 in connection with alleged falsified records regarding checks on Epstein's housing unit.

## Timeline Anchor

- **Nov 19, 2019**: SDNY announced charges against two officers (Noel and Michael Thomas) tied to recordkeeping on Aug 9-10, 2019.

## Why This Matters

- Officer-record integrity is central to understanding custody failures around Epstein's death.
- This case is a key bridge between timeline anomalies and formal legal action.
