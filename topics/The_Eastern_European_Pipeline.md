# The Eastern European Pipeline

> **Status**: **DOCUMENTED** (2026 Revelations)
> **Evidence Level**: **HIGH** - FBI records, financial transfers, visa records, emails
> **Key Sources**: Treasury files, DOJ release, Senator Wyden disclosure

## Summary

The January 2026 document release revealed the existence of a systematic **Eastern European trafficking pipeline** that funneled women and girls from countries including Poland, Belarus, Ukraine, Russia, and others to Jeffrey Epstein's properties. This operation involved visa sponsorship, wire transfers totaling over **$1 billion**, and a network of "scouts" identifying recruits in European cities.

## The Visa Sponsorship Scheme (2004-2005)

### FBI Discovery

In 2019, the FBI investigated Epstein's phone number and discovered he was listed as the **point of contact** for visa applicants seeking entry to the United States.

### Countries of Origin

Applicants sponsored by Epstein came from:
- **Poland**
- **Belarus**
- **Ukraine**
- **Russia**
- **China**
- **Croatia**
- **Slovakia**

### Visa Type

The applicants were seeking **non-immigrant visas**, suggesting:
- Temporary entry for work, tourism, or study
- No path to permanent residency
- Easier to traffic and control

### The Pattern

This revelation suggests Epstein used legitimate visa processes to bring women into the United States, providing a veneer of legality to trafficking operations.
