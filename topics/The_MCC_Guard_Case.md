# The MCC Guard Case (2019)

> **Status**: **DOCUMENTED CRIMINAL CASE (RECORD-FALSIFICATION CHARGES)**

## Summary

In November 2019, SDNY announced charges against two MCC correctional officers tied to alleged falsification of records about required inmate checks in the period surrounding Epstein's death.

## Case Core

- Defendants: Tova Noel and Michael Thomas.
- Allegation: falsified records about required rounds/checks on Epstein's housing unit.
- Case significance: provides a formal legal record of custody-process failures.

## Why It Matters

- Establishes a documented chain of misconduct allegations in jail operations.
- Distinguishes formal case facts from speculative narratives.
- Anchors later oversight findings in the OIG custody review.
