# The Zorro Ranch Commission (New Mexico)

> **Status**: **STATE-LEVEL INVESTIGATIVE PROCESS (APPROVED FEB 2026)**

## Summary

In February 2026, Reuters reported New Mexico lawmakers approved a commission process to investigate allegations tied to Epstein's Zorro Ranch. In this repository, the commission marks a shift from federal release disputes to state-level investigative follow-through.

## Timeline

- **Feb 17, 2026**: Reuters reported commission approval by New Mexico lawmakers.
- **Planned output (reported)**: hearings phase and a final report in 2027.

## Why This Matters

- Expands accountability process beyond DOJ publication and congressional disputes.
- Creates a venue focused on place-based allegations and local historical records.
- Could generate primary hearing/testimony artifacts not yet in current ingest set.
